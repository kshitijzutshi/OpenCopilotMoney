Welcome to OpenCopilotMoney! This comprehensive guide will help you get up and running with our powerful personal finance platform.

## What is OpenCopilotMoney?

OpenCopilotMoney is a modern personal finance platform that helps you track, categorize, and optimize your spending. Whether you're managing a personal budget or family finances, OpenCopilotMoney provides the tools you need to manage your money effectively.

## Key Features

- **Transaction Tracking**: Automatically import and categorize your transactions
- **Predictive Budgeting**: AI-powered budget recommendations based on your habits
- **Financial Health Score**: Get a holistic view of your financial wellbeing
- **Family Accounts**: Collaborate on finances with your family
- **Real-time Insights**: Get instant alerts and analysis of your spending

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account

Sign up for a free account at [opencopilotmoney.tech](https://opencopilotmoney.tech). You can use your email address or sign in with your GitHub account.

### 2. Connect Your Bank

Once you're logged in, you can connect your bank accounts securely:

```javascript
// Example of how OpenCopilotMoney handles transaction data (simplified)
const transactions = await opencopilot.getTransactions({
  accountId: 'YOUR_ACCOUNT_ID',
  startDate: '2024-01-01',
});

const analysis = await opencopilot.analyzeSpending(transactions);
console.log(analysis.topCategories);
```

### 3. Review Your Insights

Monitor your financial performance with detailed analytics:

- Spending trends by category
- Savings rate over time
- Future cash flow predictions
- Automated budget alerts

## Best Practices

1. **Review Categorization**: Regularly check AI categorizations to ensure accuracy
2. **Set Savings Goals**: Create specific goals to track your progress
3. **Use Family Sharing**: Collaborate with family members for a complete financial picture
4. **Enable Alerts**: Stay informed about unusual spending or budget limits
